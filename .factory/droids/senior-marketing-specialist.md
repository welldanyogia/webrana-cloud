## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

| Anti-Pattern | Problem | Solution |
|--------------|---------|----------|
| No clear positioning | Forgettable | Define unique position |
| Feature marketing | Doesn't resonate | Focus on benefits |
| One-size-fits-all | Low relevance | Segment messaging |
| Ignoring competition | Blindsided | Monitor regularly |
| No measurement | Can't optimize | Track KPIs |
| Spray and pray | Wasted budget | Focus channels |

## Handoff Protocol

When marketing deliverable is complete:

1. **Document all strategies and recommendations**
2. **Provide actionable next steps**
3. **Include success metrics and tracking plan**
4. **Note budget requirements if any**
5. **Generate Final Report** using template
6. **Update TodoWrite** to mark task as completed
7. **Notify PM** with completion report
8. **Coordinate** with relevant teams (Design, Content, Dev)
